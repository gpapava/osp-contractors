Our Company

OSP Contractors is a rapidly growing telecom infrastructure company delivering design, implementation and maintenance services for fixed telecom networks.

Today, OSP Contractors occupies more than 100 employees and provides services all over Greece with offices and warehouses in Athens and Trikala.

We deliver hundreds of fiber home installations per month and dozens kilometers of turn-key infrastructures (trenches, conduits, cables) ready to be connected.

Our goal is to adapt to the needs of each client, aiming to meet their requirements and conditions providing high-quality services. To achieve that our philosophy is not to use subcontractors to perform any of our services, but only utilize our own company resources.

Our Company

Built on Decades of Expertise

OSP Contractors is a rapidly growing telecom infrastructure company delivering design, implementation and maintenance services for fixed telecom networks. Established in 2014, we represent the natural evolution of founder Apostolos Mpesiris's prior enterprise, which spent over four decades in telecom infrastructure.

Our highest objective is to fully satisfy client requirements and deliver superior-quality services. We are completely self-sufficient, supporting our own Engineering, Trenching, Cable Blowing, Cable Recovery and Splicing crews — we never use subcontractors.
