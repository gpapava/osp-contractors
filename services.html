Our Services

End-to-end telecom infrastructure — designed, built and certified by our own crews

OSP Contractors is a rapidly growing telecom infrastructure company delivering design, implementation and maintenance services for fixed telecom networks. All work is carried out by our own specialist crews — we never use subcontractors.

Engineering Services

We can offer full engineering services to take every fixed telecom project from inception to completion. Our engineering capabilities include:

  • Building surveys for new service routing and design
  • Network infrastructure management software implementation
  • Fibre optic network designs
  • Emergency restoration planning
  • Drafting and as-built documentation

Cable Installation

Our specialised crews are able to finish any cable installation project in the most professional manner. Meticulous planning combined with years of experience and well-trained personnel ensures that our cable installations are carried out safely, diligently and on time.

Services range from labour-only through to complete turnkey solutions with full project management, covering all types of fixed telecom cable infrastructure.

Civil Works

We offer in-depth experience of civil engineering works and operate to superlative safety standards, bringing high standards of planning, safety and customer service to every site.

Our civil works capabilities include excavation, reinstatement and associated civil engineering works in any surface, with a primary focus on telecommunications projects.

Cable Recovery

We offer turn-key asset recovery services for any Telco Provider. As fibre networks expand across Greece, old copper networks tend to become operationally redundant — yet they remain a valuable asset.

Our cable recovery crews safely dismantle and recover copper and other legacy cable infrastructure, maximising the residual value for our clients.

Customer Premises Installation

We specialise in providing turnkey video, voice and data services to a variety of facilities including:

  • Apartment homes and residential buildings
  • Hotels and hospitality venues
  • Assisted living centres and healthcare facilities
  • Office buildings
  • Resorts and leisure venues

Our CPI service covers the design, build and activation of fibre, digital and coax networks within customer premises from end to end.

Splicing & Certification

Fibre optic termination, splicing, testing and maintenance are critical services required at every location that includes fibre. We provide fibre optic services in any environment, as well as solutions to improve existing infrastructure.

Fibre Optic Termination

Connection of fibre to devices, with protective measures against contamination and physical damage to ensure long-term signal integrity.

Fibre Splicing

Joining of fibre cables using precision fusion splicing methods, producing minimal signal loss and a highly reliable connection.

Testing & Certification

High-precision testing of optical fibre systems to certify performance, identify faults and validate installation quality to industry standards.
