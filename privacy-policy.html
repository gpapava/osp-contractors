Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data

Last updated: June 2025

1. Data Controller

OSP Contractors is the data controller responsible for your personal data. We are registered in Greece and operate offices in Athens and Trikala. You can contact us at info@osp-contractors.gr.

2. Data We Collect

When you contact us via the enquiry form or by email, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • The content of your message

3. Legal Basis and Purpose

We process your personal data on the basis of your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. We may also process data on the basis of our legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) where necessary to manage our business operations.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, and in any event no longer than required by applicable law. Enquiry data is typically retained for up to 3 years.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g. email hosting), subject to appropriate contractual protections.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — to request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction — to request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — to receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — to withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@osp-contractors.gr. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (HDPA) at www.dpa.gr.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or analytics cookies. No personal data is collected automatically when you browse our site.
